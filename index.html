Activities

Ocean Atlantic Rentals - daily & weekly rentals, bikes, beach chairs, umbrellas, beach equipment (also have daily setup service), surfboards/kayaks, baby equipment, golf carts
Causeway Watersports - parasailing, jet skis, & boat rentals
Jockey's Ridge State Park - highest sand dune east of the Mississippi River, free parking, walk up and watch an AMAZING sunrise or sunset!!
Wright Brother's National Memorial - tour the museum documenting where the first ever flight took place!!
The Lost Colony - in Manteo on Roanoke Island. Outdoor play documenting Sir Walter Raleigh's final expedition.

Fresh Catch Seafood

Austin's Fish Company is the one and only stop you need to make!! They have just about any seafood you can think of!! All their seafood is locally caught, probably in the previous 24 hrs. so its crazy fresh! They also steam/spice shrimp, a variety of steamer buckets to go, just call ahead as they have a daily time cut off depending on the volume of orders they get. (April - September only)
O'Neal's Sea Harvest is open year round

TV Operation

Use the Xumo remote to turn on all TVs and the cable boxes. Select the Spectrum app to watch live cable tv channels.
Select your favorite streaming app from the Xumo home screen and use your personal account/login to watch them.
Remember to log out of all apps when you leave at the end of your stay.
If the above doesn't work, use the TV remote to ensure the correct HDMI input is selected.

Temperature Control

In the SUMMER MONTHS (May - September), please close the blinds/curtains in the master bedroom and family room when you leave the condo for any extended times. This will help keep the condo cooler. Please close the blinds/curtains when you check out in the Summer.
In the WINTER MONTHS, leaving the curtains/blinds open will take advantage of natural solar heating. Please leave them open when you check out.
